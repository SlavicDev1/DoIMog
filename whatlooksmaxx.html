What is looksmaxxing?

Looksmaxxing refers to the practice of making efforts to enhance one's physical appearance
through various means. The term is often associated with the online communities that focus
on self-improvement and aesthetics. The goal of looksmaxxing is to maximize one's attractiveness
by addressing aspects such as grooming, fashion, fitness, and sometimes even cosmetic procedures.
Here are some common aspects of looksmaxxing:

-Skincare

-Nutrition

-Fitness

-Sleep

-Hygene

-Style

-Hairstyles

-Mewing
